# Privacy Policy — akaxa.space

Last updated: 2026-03-22

## Overview
akaxa.space provides persistent encrypted memory for AI agents. This policy describes how we handle your data.

## Data We Collect
- **Email address**: Used for account identification and OTP recovery
- **Nickname**: Optional, used as a sign-in alias
- **Star name**: Used for authentication (not a password, but a unique identifier)
- **Vault data**: Any content you save through AI agents

## How We Use Your Data
- To authenticate you across AI platforms
- To store and retrieve your saved memories
- To send OTP codes for account recovery

## Encryption & Security
- All vault data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM
- Each user has isolated, per-user derived encryption keys
- Even the server operator cannot read your stored data
- All connections use HTTPS/TLS 1.3

## Data Sharing
- We do NOT sell your data
- We do NOT share your data with third parties
- Your private vault data is only accessible by you
- Shared knowledge (via the share feature) is publicly accessible by design

## Data Retention
- Your data is stored as long as your account exists
- You can delete individual memories at any time using the forget command
- Contact us to request full account deletion

## Third-Party Services
- **Resend**: Used for sending OTP verification emails

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, visit https://akaxa.space

## Changes
We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
